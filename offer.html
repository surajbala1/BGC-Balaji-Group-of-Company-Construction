Quality Services

Overview



With worldwide operations spanning various countries across continents, BGC
Projects delivers comprehensive inspection, certification, and training services for
industrial and infrastructure sectors encompassing power, hydrocarbons, metals
and minerals, roads, ports and buildings, and other sectors. Quality
management is a critical facet and therefore the company is into ISO 9001
Certification, Energy audits, ERDMP, T4S, IMS Audits (PNGRB), Inspection Body
Services, Warehouse inspection (WDRA), Safety audits, customised second party
audits, and training services for the industrial sector.

The company has recently expanded into next-gen technology-enabled
services, comprising asset integrity management, project quality management,
and drone inspection services. Further additional schemes such as the STAR
scheme for security agencies and other QCI schemes are being offered. Besides,
the business unit has created a new certification services subsidiary called TQ
Cert Services Private Limited, which also caters to the emerging areas of Agri,
food, Ayurveda, and yoga. We also provide training services for infrastructure
organizations.

The business unit serves more than 1000 clients across 40 countries and is
reputed for its agility, responsiveness, on-time high-quality service delivery.

We are raising the bar for quality across the industry by ensuring training services
for the industrial and infrastructure sectors.

Operation, Renovation & Maintenance

Overview



The BGC Projects’ operation, renovation, and maintenance business serves some
of the largest operating blast furnaces in India and also expanding into new
areas and geographies. With our high-quality blast furnace in India, we help
companies operating in the metallurgy industry to enhance efficiency and
productivity.
We focus on ensuring that the plant delivers best–in–class productivity with
utmost safety, thus reducing the total cost of ownership. Drawing on our wide
experience of the EPC projects and equipment specific knowledge like blast
furnaces in India, combined with trained people and global sourcing, we provide
advanced services and solutions, customized to meet the client's specific needs.
Our maintenance service in the construction industry assists clients to prolong
the life of their structure. With our quick and efficient maintenance service in the
construction, we ensure that quality is delivered to clients.

The company caters to a wide range of industries like iron and steel, non-ferrous,
power plants, oil and hydrocarbon, petrochemicals, fertilizer, and cement. Along
with a strong standard in maintenance service in construction, BGC Projects has
become a national leader in operation, renovation & maintenance services.
