Power

Overview



BGC Projects provides end-to-end services in power generation, transmission,
power distribution solution, and smart grid management solution

BGC Projects provides customized turnkey solutions for thermal power plants
such as total EPC, comprehensive Balance of Plant (BOP), and Flue-gas
desulfurization (FGD) solutions. With rich experience in coal and gas-based
plants, super and subcritical technologies, we have executed several large and
complex projects, including India's largest power plant of its type at
Krishnapatnam.

Our expertise in planning and executing power distribution solutions makes us
the preferred choice to lay down power lines. BGC Projects has laid more than
13,000 kms of power transmission lines since its inception. As a power distribution
solution, it operates in all types of terrains, such as the Himalayan Ranges, forests,
and deserts, to install high voltage lines ranging from 440 to 765 kV to up to 1200
kV DC. BGC Projects uses the latest technology such as stringing with the help of
Drones, Material transfer with the help of Helicrane & Ropeways, smart grid
management solutions, and 3D engineering for the construction of Substation
Control room buildings to accelerate the construction of transmission lines in hilly

areas. Our strength lies in having skilled resources, robust processes, and exceptional
project management capability. This allows us to effectively monitor and control
every stage of the execution, and deliver the project on time, making us the
preferred choice for power distribution solutions and smart grid management
solutions.

Water

Overview



Water is the lifeline of industrial activity and urban conglomerates. This requires
setting up of dedicated water treatment plants, wherein, BGC Projects offers its
vast expertise and services as a top notch water treatment plant construction
company in India.

BGC Projects offers end-to-end industrial and municipal water treatment
solutions across the value chain; river rejuvenation, water supply system, sewage
system, treatment plants including desalination and effluent treatment Plants.
The company undertakes major irrigation projects on design-build basis. It
capitalizes on its in-house design, technology partnership and real time project
monitoring. It also focuses on circular economy and provides unique solutions to
various customers in converting waste water into portable and industrial water.

Recently, BGC Projects secured orders in Bulk Water supply, Sewage Treatment
Plants and Distribution Network from governmental agencies which include first
of its kind tertiary treatment plant at Vizag Smart city, water supply systems at
Bhind, Makronia and Sagar at MP and Ramnagara in Karnataka. It also successfully
diversified through the Seawater Intake project at NPCIL, Kudankulam and Micro
irrigation projects at Hanota and Banda.

Some of the noteworthy solutions created include mobile RO plants for disaster
management, IoT enablement for remote control and monitoring, nano coated
jerry cans, ozonators and SMS based pump starters.

BGC Projects also has the distinction of executing the first-of-its-kind river
Rejuvenation project in India.

Metals
& Minerals

Overview



A leading end-to-end, customized solutions provider for Ferrous and Non Ferrous
industries, BGC Projects uses cutting-edge technologies acquired through
relationships with leading technology providers. The use of a bulk material
handling system and pipe conveyor system greatly helps increase our efficiency.

BGC Projects is present across the value chain of iron and steel making and has
expertise in executing bulk material handling systems, ore beneficiation plants,
pelletisation plants, coke oven batteries, large and complex blast furnaces and
casters. With the additional use of an innovative bulk material handling system,
resources are better managed and stored. In Non Ferrous, we provide Pyro
Metallurgical and Hydro Metallurgical solutions for extraction of Copper,
Aluminium, Zinc, Lead, Titanium and precious metals. We also provide solutions
for coal washeries, pipe conveyors and raw material handling solutions for
industries, mines and ports. The implementation of an effective pipe conveyor
system ensures that metal products are transferred quickly and efficiently, with
minimum loss.

BGC Projects has a dedicated centre of Engineering Excellence (CoEE ) for metals
and minerals. This, coupled with our execution capability, enables us to provide
effective end to end solutions and deliver projects on time.

Oil & Gas

Overview



BGC Projects offers cutting-edge solutions in Oil, Gas, and Hydrocarbons to the
Indian and Middle Eastern markets. As a top oil and gas solutions company, we
execute mid and downstream projects including refinery expansion, tank farms
and terminals, onshore field development, and process plants. We have the
ability to design, build, and deliver the best solutions on time while maintaining
the highest safety and quality standards. We are also India’s leading refinery
expansion company, expanding our service to new and existing oil refineries. The
company has also entered into upcoming markets, which include 2G Bioethanol
and High technology works like turnkey delivery of refinery process Units such as
CDU/VDU, Delayed Coker Unit (DCU), and Vacuum Gas Oil (VGO).

Recently, the company received multiple mega orders in the oil & gas refinery
sector with a combined value totaling more than Rs. 6000 crore. Out of these,
three orders are from HPCL Rajasthan Refinery Ltd (HRRL) and one from Bharat
Petroleum.

BGC Projects also has a majority stake in Artson Engineering Ltd., a company with
multi-disciplinary capability in fabrication and engineering in Hydrocarbon
processing. This adds to our expertise as an oil and gas solutions company.

Transportation

Overview



BGC Projects is a leading company in India’s metro rail and airport construction
sectors, wherein it provides comprehensive solutions for the transportation
sector. It is currently building several elevated & underground metro rail
packages, highways, expressways, and a dedicated freight corridor. As a leading
infrastructure development company, BGC Projects is spearheading the
augmentation of public and Cargo transportation across India.

BGC Projects is proficient in designing and executing integrated packages which
involve the construction of embankments, flexible & rigid pavements, drainage,
elevated & underground stations, airport buildings, MEP, structures, tunnels, track
works, overhead electrification, traction substations, signaling and telecommunication
works. Currently, BGC Projects is executing 2000 track km of civil & track works
for Dedicated Freight Corridors, and 1200 route km of overhead Railway Electrification
works. BGC Projects has distinguished itself as a top airport construction
company. The company has gained respect by building the terminal building of
Bamrauli airport in Prayagraj in a record time of 11 months.

Handling more projects and completing them in record time with top quality has
made BGC Projects a leading metro rail and airport construction company. In the
recent past, the company has been awarded three important EPC projects such as
Guna Gwalior, Parkhanhatti Miraj, Solapur Gulbarga of Railway Electrification
works with a cumulative length of 707 TKM by Rail Vikas Nigam Limited and Power
Gird Corporation of India Limited.

Urban-Infrastructure

Overview



Urban infrastructure development is the foundation of every city and remains the
key to ensuring basic services like water, sanitation, drainage, energy, and
transport. With proper and planned urban infrastructure development, residents
can enjoy better living conditions & live healthier lifestyles while benefiting from
enhanced environmental sustainability. BGC Projects is investing in urban
infrastructure development to bring better standards of living to people while
simultaneously innovating with urban infrastructure development standards.

BGC Projects is proficient in mega infrastructure projects such as IT SEZ Parks,
commercial and office buildings, high rise segment, commercial retail spaces,
hospitals, hotels, data centers, warehouses, bridges, airports, and various projects
under the Smart Cities’ initiative led by the Government of India.

Currently, BGC Projects is executing marquee projects such as the BDD Chawl
rehabilitation project at Mumbai’s Worli area; and construction of a critical
package of Mumbai's Trans Harbour Link. In addition, it is undertaking smart
lighting solutions in Noida, Nashik & Ludhiana - the company is currently also
executing work on two separate elevated packages and one underground
package of Mumbai Metro Rail. It is also undertaking work on Pune’s prestigious
underground & elevated metro rail sections.
